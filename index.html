About

Hello y'all, let me introduce myself. I'm a software engineer working for MemCachier, and a "digital nomad" (read: hermit) currently residing in Brooklyn. I grew up in Arkansas, and followed that up with several years in DC before settling in good ol' New York. As a result I have developed strong feelings about BBQ, a passion for non-profit work, and a love of programming. When not working, I can be found playing ping pong, dancing, painting, or reading Terry Pratchett.

Yes, I am self-taught. Yes, I was primarily motivated by the gap I saw in tech that I knew I could fill. Yes, I'm actually putting my anthropology major to good use. Yes, I work primarily in Haskell. No, I've never been Trick-or-Treating, please stop asking.
